About the channel

Goal

Our channel is about spreading awareness about less fortunate communites through documentaries.

Target Audience

Our target audience consists of teens, adults, and teens, adults and movie enthusiasts. That's because according to our research, most teens who watch documentaries are Asians and are favored both by men and women. Adults of 18-25 years old are most likely to watch documentaries. Most of them are Asian women. Lastly, movie enthusiasts of 16-29 years olds like watching documentaries.

Why we chose documentaries

We chose to do it in documentaries because we think that documentaries give valuable content that people like to watch and be captivated by. Documentaries deal exclusively with facts and real-life events and the main purpose of a documentary is to inform and educate. Another reason why we chose documentaries is because of the needs of our target audience and we assume this is what they need. Teens need to be informed and enlightened of the recent happenings. Adults need to be engaged in these kinds of conflicts. Movie enthusiasts need to entertain themselves from their work/studies.



Our journey making a YouTube channel


Our journey begins in quarter one where we got to choose the type of content that we want to make. Then in quarter two, our groupings were changed forcing us to redecide which type of content we are going to make again. At first, we decided on educational videos but eventually ended up with documentaries. We did try to switch our type of content again but we still ended up choosing to make documentaries.


After choosing our type of content, we then had to decide on our brand. Basically, we had to decide on the name and logo of our channel. Here is where trouble happens since we don't have any creative minds, I decided to just generate random names and we will just pick which one we like. That's how we got our name. For the logo, one of our members was a "graphic designer" so they were assigned with making the logo.


Finally, after all of that, we began to plan our first video. The video was about Sitio Bebe, a fictional place that our teachers came up with as a school project for us to do. Though it was fictional, it was a good first topic for our video. Since we already had some information for it and we said "Why not? It's our first video plus it might be good practice for us". Then we started to make plans for the video. We made a storyboard and a script for the video, we also assigned roles to some of the members. After making the plans, we then proceeded to record the video and then edit it. After making some final changes and a thumbnail for the video, we then uploaded it to YouTube.







After some time after uploading the first video, we then made plans for the second video. At first, we didn't know what topic to talk about in the video since we didn't plan that far ahead. But we eventually landed on the idea of talking about the Willow Project. Then we made a storyboard, and a script, and assigned tasks to members just like in the first video. But it was somewhat different from the first video. In the last video, we recorded footage while in the second video, we planned to just do an entire video that is full of voiceovers. We knew that this would be more challenging to edit but we continued with the idea and recorded the voiceovers. After recording, our editor edited the video, and the video was revised a lot before eventually getting it into a state that we liked. Our graphic designer then made a thumbnail and we uploaded the second video to YouTube.
