FAQ

Q: Isn't your intro video just the netflix animation with your logo on it?

A: Yes

Q: Why do you have two kinds of logos?

A: The regular logo is just the regular logo but the round logo was made so that the entire logo can fit in the channel icon on youtube. Since youtube uses round channel icons, we wanted to make sure that the logo can fit inside that circle.

Q: What type of documentaries will you be making?

A: The type of documentaries that we will be making are documentaries that show the current state of a community that needs help. Showing the problems that the community is facing, what are the effects of those problems, and how the community is dealing with those problems.

Q: How can this channel help communities?

A: This channel can help communities by spreading awareness about their current situation. If we spread enough awareness, more people will come and recognize the situation and try to help the communities.

Q: Why did you guys choose make documentaries instead of something else?

A: There are two reasons for that. The first reason is because this is what we assume they need (for more information check the about page) and the second reason is because we can't think of anything else to make and we just reused some of our old research for this channel.
